About BCPEI

The Boy Child Protection and Empowerment Initiative (BCPEI) is a dedicated organization committed to safeguarding and nurturing the well-being of boys in our communities. We recognize that boys face unique challenges including abuse, neglect, violence, and lack of positive role models.

Through comprehensive programs in advocacy, education, mentorship, and psychosocial support, we work tirelessly to create safe spaces where boys can thrive, develop their full potential, and become responsible leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To build a society where every boy child is safe, confident, respected, and empowered to reach his full potential without fear, intimidation, or neglect.

Our Mission

To protect, nurture, and empower the boy child through advocacy, education, mentorship, psychosocial support, and community engagement, ensuring equal opportunities for growth and development.

What We Do

Our comprehensive programs address the unique needs of the boy child

Boy Child Protection & Advocacy

We advocate for the rights and protection of boys, raising awareness about issues affecting them and working with communities to create safer environments.

Mentorship and Role-Modeling

Connecting boys with positive male role models who guide, inspire, and support their personal development and character building.

Educational Support & Life Skills

Providing academic support, scholarships, and essential life skills training to help boys succeed in school and beyond.

Mental Health & Psychosocial Support

Offering counseling, therapy, and support groups to address emotional and psychological challenges faced by boys.

Community Awareness & Outreach

Engaging communities through workshops, seminars, and campaigns to change perceptions and create supportive environments for boys.

Policy Advocacy for Child Rights

Working with policymakers and stakeholders to develop and implement policies that protect and promote the rights of the boy child.

Meet Our Founder

Robert Wafula Okello

Founder & Executive Director

A passionate child rights advocate dedicated to protecting and empowering the boy child through education, mentorship, and community-based initiatives. Robert's vision and commitment have been instrumental in establishing BCPEI as a leading voice for boy child rights and welfare.

With years of experience in community development and child protection, Robert continues to lead the organization's mission to create safe, nurturing environments where every boy can thrive and reach his full potential.
